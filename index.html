Athlete Guide

Focus on your craft. We'll handle the funding.

What We Offer

CrowdAthletes allows you to raise capital from global investors to cover your living expenses, professional trainers, and equipment. You don't have to work an additional job; you can invest 100% of your time into yourself and your sport.

The Deal

During the investment period, you are not expected to yield returns. The funds are yours to use for your athletic development as outlined in your Masterplan.

When you make the jump to the professional level later on, you will pay back a pre-agreed percentage of your future contract and sponsorship earnings to your investors through our platform.

Who we are looking for

We are seeking highly-driven, elite amateur or aspiring professional athletes who have a proven track record of regional or national success but lack the financial backing to turn pro. You must be willing to verify your identity and performance statistics with our team.

Investor Guide

Back the next generation of superstars.

What is CrowdAthletes?

CrowdAthletes is a unique marketplace where you can invest directly into the future earnings of high-potential athletes.

How it works

Athletes need funding today to pay for elite coaching, travel, and living expenses so they can focus entirely on reaching the professional leagues. In exchange for your capital, athletes sign a Smart Contract committing a percentage of their future professional earnings back to the platform.

Secondary Market

You do not have to wait years for a payout. As an athlete performs well and their verification score rises, the perceived value of their future contract increases. You can trade your shares with other investors on our Secondary Market.
